Key Details

Minimap
  • Thin strip above the timeline
  • Shows all tracks as colored bars
  • Red viewport rectangle = visible area
  • Drag viewport to scroll timeline
  • Click anywhere to jump to that position
Loop Region
  • Green highlighted region on ruler
  • Drag on ruler in Select mode to define
  • Loop checkbox in transport activates it
  • Playhead bounces between L and R markers
  • Click ruler in Cursor mode to seek
